About Us

We've been making healthy food last for 10 years

Hello, I am Anand Kumar, the founder and owner of “AK Chettinadu Hotel” Chennai’s most popular biryani brand. I’ve been working in the restaurant and hotel industry for over 15 years now. As a native of Chennai, I’ve always have been a huge fan of traditional Tamil wedding style biryani. I’ve been attending many friends and family weddings since childhood, only just to relish some authentic, super-delicious wedding style biryani, and truly for nothing else!
